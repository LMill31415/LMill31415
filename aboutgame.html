Game Mechanics

Some of the game mechanics that you may have is a bank there are also 3 bosses at the moment. The first boss is like any starting boss, pretty easy, but after that first boss the difficulty increases. There is also a 4th boss but I don't include it because it doesn't achieve anything right now, but in the future it will lead to a new area of the world.

Gameplay

The gameplay is like your average rpg where you start at your house and continue on from there. Your goal is to get to school and to do that you are given items that will help you on your journey. There are NPCs that will either help you or slow down your journey to get to school. The game has a strong difficulty curve but if you put in time and grind the bosses won't be too much of a difficulty for you.
